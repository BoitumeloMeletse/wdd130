Our History

Founded in the early 2010s by a group of enthusiastic rafters, our company was created with the goal of sharing the thrill of whitewater rafting with adventurers worldwide. What began as a modest, local operation offering guided tours on the region's wild and scenic rivers has since grown into one of the country’s premier rafting companies. Today, we proudly offer a variety of trips suited to all ages and skill levels, making unforgettable experiences accessible to everyone.

Our dedicated team of expert guides is passionate about creating exceptional rafting adventures on some of the world’s most stunning rivers. From the exhilarating rapids of the Tikal Gorge to the tranquil beauty of the Highland Falls, we offer something for every adventurer. For us, rafting is not just about adrenaline; it’s about immersing yourself in nature, discovering hidden landscapes, and making memories that will last a lifetime.
